Reversing Metabolic Diseases

September 30th, 2023

One thing that might come as a shock to most people is the idea that metabolic diseases like: Atherosclerosis, Diabetes and High Blood Pressure can be reversed. I know I certainly was when I first came across this idea. What might be even harder to believe is that these metabolic diseases can be reversed through diet and lifestyle changes!

When looking at the epidemiology of these diseases, it quickly becomes apparent that areas where the Standard American Diet is commonplace have the highest incidence of these metabolic diseases. Furthermore, areas where metabolic diseases occur the least (such as rural China and parts of Africa) typically has a diet centered around vegetables, fruits, legumes and grains.

Furthermore when looking at the works of Dr. Dean Ornish as well as Dr. Caldwell Esselstyn, these physicians were able to prove that not only was it possible to halt the progression of metabolic diseases in it's tracks, but also reverse the progression of these diseases to improve quality of life! They were able to accomplish these incredible feats by having patients change and stick to a Plant-Based Diet.

How is this possible?

Generally, common knowledge dictates that heart disease is caused by high cholesterol in the blood. Low Density Lipo-proteins embed into the arterial walls forming plaques which harden over time reducing overall blood circulation. By reducing overall cholesterol in the blood, it's possible to stop the progression of heart disease.

Type 2 Diabetes is caused by insulin resistance in which the cells that require insulin to make use of the glucose in the blood stops responding to the insulin released by the Pancreas. The reason this happens is due to a high fat diet which creates an environment in the blood that prevents insulin from reaching the cells that require it to make use of the glucose in the blood. By reducing fat intake in the diet, specifically Saturated Fats and Trans Fats, the body can begin to restore homeostasis within these cells and increase insulin sensitivity allowing glucose to be used by the cells once again.

And wouldn't you know, the Standard American Diet happens to contain high dietary cholesterol consumption as well as high saturated fats and trans fats. Animal products promoted by the Standard American Diet have been shown to increase cholesterol in the blood leading to Atherosclerosis. Furthermore, fast food and diary products tend to contain high amounts of Saturated Fats as well as Trans Fats which contribute to insulin resistance and eventually type 2 Diabetes.

Why plant-based?

Unprocessed, organic, plant-based foods are typically low in Saturated fats and generally contain zero Trans fats. They also don't contain dietary cholesterol and consuming dietary cholesterol has been shown to increase serum cholesterol in the blood.

In addition to removing the conditions for metabolic diseases to form in the first place, fruits and vegetables also contain anti-oxidants which prevents further damage from oxidation in the body and allows the body to heal itself.

Through the combination of a healthy plant-based diet along with regular exercise, the metabolic diseases of the west are no longer a lifetime sentence of pharmaceuticals delaying the inevitable. Instead they can be overcome through focused effort on your diet and lifestyle.
