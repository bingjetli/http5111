Aside from that, I'm currently self-studying French. Here's a short snippet from the notes I've written already on it.

Adverbs are words that modify verbs, adjectives or another adverb.

In French, adverbs typically follow the active verb in the sentence.

Adverbs are typically created using a base descriptive adjective, followed by a specific suffix ending:

  • feminine base adjective : append -ment
  • masculine base adjective, vowel ending : append -ment
  • masculine base adjective, -ent or -ant ending : append -emment or -amment

NOTE: The adverbs bon and mauvais have irregular adverbs : bien and mal.
